# Why Deal-Safer? — Logic Tree (Why Tree)

## Root Product: Deal-Safer (contract defense system)

---

## Why Up: The Underlying Desires

```
[Deal-Safer]
    ↑ enables
[Negotiation Power Before Signing]
    ↑ because
[Fair Contract Outcome]
    ↑ because
[Financial Safety & Business Sustainability]
    ↑ because
[Survive & Thrive as a Small Player in an Unequal System]
```

**The core why:** People without legal resources are structurally disadvantaged in every contract negotiation. One bad clause can end a business or wipe out savings. This is a solvable, recurring, expensive problem.

---

## How Down: What Enables Each Why

### How do you get "Negotiation Power Before Signing"?
- Know which clauses are dangerous → **Poison Clause Scanner**
- Know what's missing → **Missing Clause Detector**
- Know what to ask for → **Redline Generator**
- Know how to ask → **Counter-Proposal Email Draft**

### How do you get "Fair Contract Outcome"?
- Real legal grounding (not hallucination) → **korean-law-mcp API**
- User's specific stance (not generic advice) → **Onboarding: Who Are You?**
- Adversarial framing (not neutral) → **Bodyguard Persona System**

### How do you get "Financial Safety"?
- Prevent signing bad contracts → Deal-Safer (pre-signature)
- Resolve disputes after signing → (out of scope for v1)
- Educate users on law → (secondary effect, not primary goal)

## The Compounding Loop (Why Users Return)

```
User signs a safe contract
    → tells colleague → colleague uses it
    → new contract type added
    → every contract in their life runs through Deal-Safer
```

**Why users come back every time:** Contracts don't stop. Business relationships generate new contracts continuously. Each new contract is a new use case.
